The reason I am creating this website!

The main reason I decided to create this website, was because I wanted to improve my ability in website design and creation aswell as make something that is meaningful to my family and my loved ones. At the top of the page, consists the hyperlinks to the different webpages I will create for each of my family members.

Every Page is going to have a different design, I have done this to challenge myself to explore more of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
