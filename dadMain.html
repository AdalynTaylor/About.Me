About me as a person

My name is Paul Taylor. I live in a small town called Banbury in United Kingdom.

I work as a refuge collector for our town's county council.

I have a lovely family, consisting of 2 children, one biological and one step and a lovely Wife

I first got into 3D printing when I found an almost mint condition printer in a bin when I was on one of my rounds

When I bought it home I fixed it by replacing a motor in it and it was working amazingly, although I of course had to adjust settings in it a lot throughtout the process

This website is going to be about my adventures with 3D printing and what it has done for me, aswell as some timelapses I have recorded!

I hope you enjoy your visit!
